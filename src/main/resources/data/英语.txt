# 一、选词填空题（CLOZE）
# ========================================

CLOZE|英语|The weather was so ____ that we decided to stay indoors all day. 备选词：terrible, terribly, terror, terrified|terrible|terribly|terror|terrified|A|10|EASY|此处需要形容词修饰weather，terrible表示"糟糕的"。

CLOZE|英语|She has made ____ progress in learning English over the past year. 备选词：consider, considerable, considerably, consideration|consider|considerable|considerably|consideration|B|10|MEDIUM|此处需要形容词修饰progress，considerable表示"相当大的"。

CLOZE|英语|The company is ____ for experienced software engineers. 备选词：look, looking, looked, to look|look|looking|looked|to look|B|10|EASY|is后面接现在分词构成进行时，looking for表示"寻找"。

CLOZE|英语|His speech was so ____ that many people fell asleep. 备选词：boring, bored, bore, boredom|boring|bored|bore|boredom|A|10|MEDIUM|此处需要形容词修饰speech，boring表示"令人厌烦的"，bored表示"感到厌烦的"。

CLOZE|英语|We need to ____ the problem before it gets worse. 备选词：address, dress, addresses, addressing|address|dress|addresses|addressing|A|10|MEDIUM|need to后接动词原形，address表示"处理、解决"。

CLOZE|英语|The new policy will have a significant ____ on the economy. 备选词：effect, affect, effective, effectively|effect|affect|effective|effectively|A|10|MEDIUM|此处需要名词，have an effect on表示"对...有影响"。

CLOZE|英语|Despite the difficulties, they ____ to finish the project on time. 备选词：managed, managing, manage, management|managed|managing|manage|management|A|10|MEDIUM|根据语境应使用过去时，managed to do表示"设法做成某事"。

CLOZE|英语|The teacher encouraged the students to ____ in class discussions. 备选词：participate, participation, participating, participant|participate|participation|participating|participant|A|10|EASY|encourage sb to do sth，此处需要动词原形participate表示"参与"。
